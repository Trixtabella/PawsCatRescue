Kitten Season

Kitten Season is a term used to describe the period in which cats predominantly breed and give birth.
Although cats can breed nearly all year long, Kitten Season tends to run from April to late autumn.
It's around this time of year that we see an influx of kittens into the rescue, but being a responsible owner can help us in combatting this issue.
Get in touch with us and we can offer support in getting your cats speyed and neutered.
